Labour's election chief Alan Milburn has defended his party's campaign posters amid Tory claims the ads were nothing short of "sly anti-Semitism". Labour has withdrawn two controversial posters and launched four new designs. The posters were labelled a "big misjudgement" by the Conservatives who said Labour's "first shot in the election has badly backfired".
