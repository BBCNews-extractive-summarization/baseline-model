Republic of Ireland manager Brian Kerr has been granted his wish for a home game as the final World Cup qualifier. Ireland will close their bid to reach the 2006 finals by playing Switzerland in Dublin on 12 October 2005. The Group Four fixtures were hammered out at a meeting in Dublin on Tuesday. Kerr had hoped to finish with a clash against France, but got the reigning European champions as their penultimate home match on 7 September 2005.
