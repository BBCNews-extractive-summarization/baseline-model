Olympic pole vault champion Yelena Isinbayeva has confirmed she will take part in the 2005 Norwich Union Grand Prix in Birmingham on 18 February. "Everybody knows how much I enjoy competing in Britain. "As Olympic champion there will be more attention on me this year, but hopefully I can respond with another record in Birmingham." Isinbayeva, 22, has set 10 world records in the pole vault, three of which have come on British soil.
