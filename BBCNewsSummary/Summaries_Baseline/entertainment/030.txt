Road trip comedy Sideways has had more praise heaped on it by two US critics' associations, adding to honours it has already picked up. The Chicago Film Critics Association (CFCA) named it winner in five categories including best film and best actor for Paul Giamatti. The Southeastern Film Critics also awarded Sideways its best film of the year accolade. Sideways has already been voted best film by critics associations in New York and Los Angeles and has been nominated for a Golden Globe. Michael Moore's controversial documentary Fahrenheit 9/11 won the best documentary, while A Very Long Engagement won best foreign film.
