Comic book veteran Stan Lee is to team up with producer Robert Evans to create a movie featuring a new superhero. Paramount Pictures, the studio behind the film, have revealed few details about the project but say it has the potential to spawn a series of films. Lee is best known for his work on Spider-Man and The Incredible Hulk. Many of Lee's other creations, including X-Men and Daredevil, have been turned into films in the past five years. Another Marvel Comics adaptation, The Fantastic Four, will be released in cinemas this summer.
