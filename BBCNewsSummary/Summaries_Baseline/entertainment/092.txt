US actor Jamie Foxx has been given two nominations for Golden Globe awards, with Meryl Streep, Morgan Freeman and Cate Blanchett also up for prizes. Foxx has starred in Collateral and Ray. The Golden Globes, Hollywood's second most prominent awards, are the first major nominations to be announced. Sean Penn, Charlize Theron, Tim Robbins and Renee Zellweger all won acting awards - mirroring the eventual Oscars outcome.
