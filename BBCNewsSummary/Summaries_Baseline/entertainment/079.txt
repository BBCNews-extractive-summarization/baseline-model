Russian drama The Return (Vozvrashchenie) has been named winner of the BBC Four World Cinema Award. Directed by Andrey Zvyagintsev, The Return previously won the 2003 Golden Lion award at the Venice Film Festival. Ross, who is the presenter of BBC One's Film 2005, was also involved in the deliberations. Other nominees included the Motorcycle Diaries, Zatoichi and Hero. The winner of the World Cinema Award last year was the French animated feature Belleville Rendezvous
