Cat Deeley has resigned as host of ITV1's Saturday morning children's music show CD:UK after six years. Deeley will host her last show on 5 March and guest presenters will stand in until a replacement is found. Deeley formerly co-hosted SM:tv, the children's show which airs before CD:UK on a Saturday morning, alongside hit presenters Ant and Dec. She won a Children's Bafta award for best presenter in December 2001 and also fronted BBC One's Fame Academy series.
