Delta Air Lines is cutting domestic fares by as much as 50% as part of a plan to ensure its financial survival. Delta is restructuring in a bid to fight off insolvency. Delta's new SimpliFares were trialled from August last year on tickets from Cincinnati, its second-largest hub. Delta, which lost $646m in the three months to September, was forced to cut 6,900 jobs worldwide as part of its aim to slash $5bn from its costs. Airline shares closed lower on the announcement, with Delta, Continental and American Airlines all falling by more than 7%.
